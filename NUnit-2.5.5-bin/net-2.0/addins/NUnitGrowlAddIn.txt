NUnit Add-in for Growl Notifications
http://nunitgrowladdin.codeplex.com/
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


INSTALLATION

1. Close all running NUnit instances.
2. Extract/copy files to the NUnit addins folder (typically, "C:\Program Files\<NUnit folder>\bin\net-2.0\addins").


UNINSTALLATION

1. Close all running NUnit instances.
2. Delete the following files:
   2.1. NUnitGrowlAddIn.*
   2.2. Growl.* (if you do not have another NUnit add-in using these Growl libraries).


NOTES

Add-in settings are configured in the NUnitGrowlAddIn.dll.config.
